Common Questions

You asked.
We answered.

Is All-on-4 my only option? ›
No. All-on-4 is just one of several full-arch solutions. The right option depends on your bone, your goals, and your budget — which is exactly what your consultation is for. We'll walk you through everything available to you.
Will treatment be painful? ›
Surgical treatment is performed under IV sedation — you'll feel no pain and most patients have no memory of the procedure at all. Post-op discomfort is manageable with prescribed medication.
How long does the process take? ›
It depends on the treatment that's right for you. From consultation to final restoration, full-arch reconstruction typically takes 3–6 months, and many patients receive a temporary restoration the same day as surgery.
How much does reconstruction cost? ›
Cost varies based on the option that best fits you and whether you need one or both arches. We focus on the right treatment, not the most expensive — and the best way to get accurate pricing is a free consultation. Flexible financing is available through CareCredit® and Proceed Finance.
Am I a candidate? ›
Most adults with missing or failing teeth are candidates for some form of reconstruction. Bone density, gum health, and medical history are all reviewed at your free consultation, where you'll get a clear, honest answer.
