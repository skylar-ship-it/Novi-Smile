Common Questions

You asked.
We answered.

All-on-X is a full arch restoration that replaces an entire row of teeth with a fixed, permanent prosthesis anchored by 4 strategically placed titanium implants. Unlike dentures, they never slip, need no adhesives, and function exactly like natural teeth — permanently.
Surgery is performed under IV sedation — you'll feel no pain and most patients have no memory of the procedure at all. Post-op discomfort is manageable with prescribed medication.
From consultation to final restoration, treatment typically takes 3–6 months. Many patients receive a temporary restoration the same day as surgery.
All-on-X cost varies based on your unique case and whether you need one or both arches. The best way to get accurate pricing is a free consultation with Dr. Frank — no surprises. Flexible financing available through CareCredit® and Proceed Finance.
Most adults with missing teeth are candidates. Bone density, gum health, and medical history are all reviewed at your free consultation. Dr. Frank will give you a clear answer — no surprises.
